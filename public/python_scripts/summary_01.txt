And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has denied the Plaintiff's application for default judgment without prejudice. The Plaintiff, Mediterranean Shipping Company (USA) Inc., as agent for MSC Mediterranean Shipping Company, S.A., is seeking default judgment against Yun Long Trucking Inc. for breaching a Uniform Intermodel Interchange and Facilities Access Agreement. However, the judge has noted several defects in the Plaintiff's application, including a lack of documentary evidence of the Plaintiff's standing to sue as agent, a lack of signature pages to the Access Agreements, and a failure to provide invoices summarized in Exhibit 3. The judge has directed the Plaintiff to submit a revised default judgment packet that addresses these issues.
